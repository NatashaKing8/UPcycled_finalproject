About Us

We are a clothing upcycling company who is dedicated to turning unwanted and retired pieces of clothing into fashionable modern garments. We buy unwanted garments, pieces of clothing and fabrics from people wanting to give their old clothes a new life. With the help of our talented team of fashion designers, we combine different fabrics and materials to make new, original and unique garments. We then liaise and distribute these refreshed pieces of fashion to our upcycled clothing shop partners who in turn sell these to new customers, allowing otherwise forgotten garments a new life! As a company, our main mission is to popularise and make upcycled fashion widely available to people and apparel businesses. Bringing this new wave of sustainability into mainstream will help minimise waste going into landfills, oceans and waste management facilities.
