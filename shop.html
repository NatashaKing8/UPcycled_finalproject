UPcycled Clothing Partnerships

Some of the upcycled clothing shops we partner with are:


Amour Vert

Amour Vert is a sustainable clothing brand that makes stylish, cute, and classic basics for women in the United States with a comfortable and flattering fit. Stocking discounted clothes made from natural earthy materials such as hemp, ethical wool, certified organic cotton, and other sustainable materials, Amour Vert prides itself in producing small batches of clothing and fighting mass production and consequent waste. Amour Vert, meaning green heart in French, does justice to its name by offering "green" clothing with a lot of heart and soul to it. Learn more!

Urban Outfitters

Urban Outfitters is a widely known High Street brand who, despite its fast fashion facet, has started their own upcycled program called Urban Renewal to try and offset some of the negative effects of fast fashion. The team at Urban Outfitters sorts through deadstock and surplus materials that generally end up in landfills and turn them into recycled clothing materials, giving them a new life. Urban Outfitters is working towards a combination of convenience, affordability and sustainability. Learn more!
