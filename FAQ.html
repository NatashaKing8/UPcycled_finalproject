FAQs

Why should I sell my clothes at UPcycled?

We encourage you to choose UPcycled as you would be making an environmentally green choice and helping to offset the dangerous effects of fast fashion.

What does the process look like?

Reach out to us with any clothes you want to sell. We will then buy them from you and have our team of designers create a refreshed look for them. Finally, we will distribute them to upcycled clothing stores, where these revitalised garments can begin a new cycle of life.

Partnering with UPcycled!

If you are an upcycled clothing shop and would like to partner with us, please contact us at info@UPcycled.co.uk

What is sustainable fashion?

Sustainable fashion refers to pieces of clothing, garments and fabrics that are designed, manufactured, and distributed in ways that are environmentally friendly.

What is upcycled clothing?

Upcycled clothing is the method of taking old, worn out, damaged or simply unwanted materials and repurposing them into new, original and unique garments and accessories.

What is the goal of sustainable fashion?

To lower the risk on the enviornment.
